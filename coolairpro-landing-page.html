Landing Page Design

CoolAirPro Landing Page

A conversion-focused landing page design for an AC service brand with strategic CTAs, trust-building elements, and mobile-first approach to maximize bookings.

View Prototype/File

Role: UI/UX Designer
Tools: Figma, Adobe Illustrator

Overview

CoolAirPro, a local AC service provider, needed a high-converting landing page to attract new customers. The primary goal was to increase online bookings and service inquiries through a clear, trustworthy, and mobile-friendly design.

Problem

The existing online presence was weak and not optimized for conversions. Potential customers found it difficult to quickly find service information, pricing, and a clear call-to-action, leading to high bounce rates and lost business.

Goals

  • Design a visually appealing and trustworthy landing page.
  • Optimize for mobile-first experience.
  • Implement clear and compelling calls-to-action (CTAs).
  • Showcase services and pricing transparently.
  • Build trust through customer testimonials and guarantees.

Process

The design process involved competitor analysis, defining user personas, and creating wireframes to map out the user journey. I focused on a clean layout with a strong visual hierarchy to guide users towards the primary goal of booking a service.

Final Design / Screens

Hero section with a clear headline and CTA, services section, testimonials, and a prominent booking form.

Outcome

The new landing page design resulted in a significant increase in online bookings and a lower bounce rate. The clear CTAs and trust-building elements helped convert more visitors into customers.
