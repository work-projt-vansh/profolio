SaaS Landing Page

Martech Landing Page

A conversion-optimized B2B SaaS landing page for a marketing technology platform, designed to communicate complex features while driving qualified leads.

Role: UI/UX Designer
Tools: Figma, Framer

Overview

This project focused on designing a B2B SaaS landing page for a marketing technology platform. The main objective was to generate qualified leads by clearly communicating the platform's value proposition and encouraging demo requests.

Problem

B2B SaaS products often have complex features that are difficult to communicate to a non-technical audience. The challenge was to create a landing page that was both informative and persuasive, without overwhelming potential customers.

Goals

  • Clearly articulate the product's features and benefits.
  • Optimize for lead generation and demo requests.
  • Build credibility and trust with potential customers.
  • Create a scalable design that can be used for future marketing campaigns.

Process

I started by researching the target audience and competitive landscape. I then created a content hierarchy and wireframes to structure the page. The design was brought to life with high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes in Figma and Framer, focusing on a clean aesthetic and clear calls-to-action.

Final Design / Screens

Hero section with a strong value proposition, feature highlights with visuals, social proof (customer logos, testimonials), and a prominent demo request form.

Outcome

The final landing page design successfully balanced feature communication with a clean user experience. The clear calls-to-action and trust-building elements led to an increase in demo requests and a higher conversion rate.
